What this number is, and is not

  • Clenching your jaw raises "focus" exactly like concentrating does. Beta (13–30 Hz) overlaps jaw and neck muscle activity, and one channel cannot separate them.
  • 50 is your own baseline, frozen after the first 20 s. The score is not comparable to anyone else's, and only meaningful within one session.
  • Calm is not the opposite of focus. It is the alpha share of the signal — a relaxation cue that moves independently.
  • There is no stress reading. A single around-ear channel cannot support one, so NeuroHUD does not show one.
